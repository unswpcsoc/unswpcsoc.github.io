Events

Workshops

UNSW PCSoc is dedicated to hosting frequent and interesting workshops that cater for all members. Workshops range from simple tutorials for parts selections and PC building, all the way to preparing your setup for liquid nitrogen overclocking.

Gaming

UNSW PCSoc is working in conjunction with other societies and organisations to provide members with opportunities to enjoy the best gaming experience on the PC platform.

External

UNSW PCSoc is in contact with major domestic and international organisations so that we can provide assistance to them when they wish to host events such as launch parties and showcases. It is in our interest to provide opportunities to our members to enjoy these events.

    PCSoc Keyboard Meet & BBQ

    We all know the feeling. You like keyboards. The mechanical kind. You like them a lot. There's a problem, your friends don't give a damn about the merits of each switch type, a 60% keyboard's superb space-efficiency, or even the Massdrop shipment that FINALLY arrived at your doorstep! They don't understand. But there's hope. We understand. We understand those nights spend cleaning caps and boards, scouring eBay for a Model M, salivating over JellyKeys' newest concoction of artisans, the longing scrolls through /r/MechanicalKeyboards, the wallet quaking silently as you browse soon-to-close group buys. We get it. Who are we? We're PCSoc, and we're holding a Keyboard meetup at uni!
